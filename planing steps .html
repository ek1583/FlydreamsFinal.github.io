Steps to close a flight:

  1. Preferential vacation style decision (belly-back, teaching (museums, cathedrals, etc.) winter/summer)
  2. Choosing the destination that best suits your chosen vacation
  3. Vacation budget decision
  4. Choose vacation
  5. Price comparison between flight closure sites
  6. Choosing a flight that suits your budget In addition, we recommend that you thoroughly check the conditions of the ticket before purchasing it. What does it mean?
  7. How much will I have to pay if canceled, modified or no-show. In order for me to know if the conditions of the ticket are suitable for me in addition to what comes with the ticket in terms of baggage (trolley, suitcase (if so how many kilos)
  8. Closing a hotel/apartment in a central location (not required)
  9. Purchase of travel insurance + baggage
  10. Closing a rental car (if necessary)
  11. Purchase of a surfing package in advance to the desired destination
  12. Purchasing tickets to attractions/trains in advance (not mandatory but recommended!)
  13. Planning the hazelnut on vacation (what to do every day - you can see recommendations on *our* website or on the traveler's website)
  14. Preparation of all documents for the flight (valid passport, confirmation of flight reservation, confirmation of hotel reservation, international license)
